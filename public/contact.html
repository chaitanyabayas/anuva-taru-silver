Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom jewelry design?

Yes! We specialize in creating custom silver jewelry pieces. Contact us with your ideas and we'll work with you to bring your vision to life.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in original condition. Custom pieces are made to order and cannot be returned unless there's a defect.

Do you provide jewelry repair services?

Yes, we offer repair services for silver jewelry. Bring your pieces to us or contact us for a consultation on repair options.

How long does it take to create custom pieces?

Custom jewelry typically takes 2-4 weeks to complete, depending on the complexity of the design. We'll provide a timeline when you place your order.
